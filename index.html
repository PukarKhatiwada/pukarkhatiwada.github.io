Who I am

A developer rooted
in craft & precision.

Hey, I'm Pukar — a frontend developer based in Ilam, a small hill town in eastern Nepal that's mostly known for its tea gardens and foggy mornings. I've spent enough time here that patience just sort of rubbed off on me, and it shows in how I build things.

My foundation is HTML & CSS — I like knowing the raw materials well before reaching for a framework to speed things up. Good structure and styling first, shortcuts second.

Mostly, I just want the sites I build to feel right: fast, accessible, and comfortable to use whether you're on a laptop or a five-year-old phone. Pixel-perfect landing page or a messy layout problem, I'll dig in until it's solid.
